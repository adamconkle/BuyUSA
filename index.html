About Buy USA

Buy USA helps you identify whether a product is made in the USA. Enter a barcode to check.

How to Use This App: Buy USA is an app that helps you identify whether a product is made in the USA. Simply enter a barcode to check its country of origin.

Disclaimer: While we strive for accuracy, the information provided may not always be complete or up to date. Please verify product origins through packaging labels when accuracy is important.

About Us: Buy USA is a free tool designed to help consumers identify products made in the USA. We use the Open Food Facts database to provide information about product origins.
